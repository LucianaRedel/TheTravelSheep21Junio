About us

I would like to make you feel just a little bit of the wonderful feeling of dreaming about something and then working for it every day. Even when it scares you, even when you want to give up many times because it seems impossible, just keep trying it if it makes you happy, if it is your dream, because it is worth. This can apply to traveling as well as to any daily life challenge you face. I have always wanted to see the Eifel Tower, visiting Paris was my first big dream. I remember I was walking from the train station to the Hotel, and then I saw it, far at the distance, and I still recall the feeling, my eyes filed with emotion tears, I could not believe I was finally there! It is a moment I will never forget.

Traveling for me, it is the most enriching experience, it opens your mind, it takes you out of your comfort zone, it leads you to see the world, it moves you; and when it is something you have worked really hard to achieve, I can assure you it is a double feeling of satisfaction!

We can work together to make the world a better and kinder place. We do not need much, although we tend to believe we need to be the President of the country, an influencer, a millionaire, a famous celebrity or something like that to change the world, the truth is, we all can do something from our place, we can be happier by following our dreams, we can be kinder by helping others, we can rise our children to be responsible and good citizen, we can teach them to work hard every day, we can give our best every day, we should treat others the way we want to be treated....

The contribution of every one of us can make, and it actually does, make the difference

Mission

Connecting traveling passionate people around the world, share experiences, recommendations, meet them and share a day, or a couple of hours touring around. What could be better than experiencing the place you are visiting with a local?

Vision

Find in every city someone willing to help you, to guide you, making a more friendly world. We can all feel good by doing something small for others

Values

Honesty - Hard work - Education - Keep learning every day, no matter how old you are, learning is not about age - Believe in yourself and always keep trying it - Always choose to do the right thing, no matter how difficult it can be, and no matter if someone is looking at you or not - Being kind and a good human being is important - Stay humble
