North America

Let's watch a video to get inspired about what it is, in my personal opinion, the most amazing destination in the US: New York

Well, what could I tell you about the USA that you do not know already? It is an amazing country, with beautiful places, however, I will focus on the cities that, again in my opinion, are worth visiting

New York

Known as the Big Apple, this is a city that will leave you completely marvelous: a cosmopolitan city, crowed everywhere, with so many cool places to visit and enjoy, always growing and evolving, with big iconic landmarks and towering skyscrapers. It is a city where you can experience a vibrant culture all around, with awesome neighborhoods and boroughs to walk and discover.

The city's twinkling lights and chaotic corners also invite you to embrace every New York minute, explore every place and create your own urban adventure. There are endless ways to spend your time in the city that never sleeps, but before you leave, stop and look around, because what is there today will be transformed into something bigger and better tomorrow.

Now, when it is the best moment to visit this wonderful city? The best time to visit New York is anytime. Each season the city offers visitors plenty of reasons to visit. Early fall offers crisp breezes, bright sun and comfortable temperatures while late fall and winter make merry with the Thanksgiving Day Parade at Macy’s and holiday decorations. Christmas is for sure a magic time to visit, if you want to submerge yourself into a city fully decorated and find the spirit of Christmas everywhere you go. Among many others, Rockefeller Center with its outdoor ice rink is an unforgettable experience. However, it is winter, and it might snow every day, which will complicate a little bit getting out

I have been there in September and in March, spring and autumn, and I can say it is a completely different city. Spring is glorious and New Yorkers celebrate the thaw by taking to the streets, shopping at outdoor markets. In summer days parks are full of people enjoying the green places of the city as well as the walk paths along the river. Restaurants set their tables outside in some neighborhoods and the atmosphere will really make you believe that sleeping is no longer necessary.

How to get around the city of New York? The best way to get around New York City is on foot, as traffic is heavy. That said, the subway system is a convenient option too, and it extends throughout Manhattan and into the other New York boroughs. Buses are another affordable way to get around, but keep in mind they traverse streets clogged with weaving cars and cabs, so it could delay a little to get where you want to get, but, since you are a tourist, you can just relax and enjoy every second there

What to do and see in New York?

Everything! It is a city to make no plans, to walk around, to discover, to get lost, to see everything, to visit every museum, to enter every department store, every place is unforgettable, even if you are not a city lover, this city is so incredible that you will end up not wanting to leave!

Chicago

Chicago, on Lake Michigan in Illinois, is among the largest cities in the US. Famed for its bold architecture, it has a skyline punctuated by skyscrapers such as the iconic John Hancock Center, Willis Tower (formerly the Sears Tower) and the neo-Gothic Tribune Tower. The city is also renowned for its museums, including the Art Institute of Chicago with its noted Impressionist and Post-Impressionist works.

Things to do here

a. Navy Pier

b. Willis and Tribune Tower

c. Navigate the Chicago River

d. Walk along their streets, especially Michigan Avenue

e. Field museum of national history

f. 360 observation deck

g. Millennium Park

h. Chicago riverwalk

i. Legoland discovery

j. Sightseeing cruises along Michigan Lake

Boston

Explore the city where the new and the old are in constant conversation, where cobbled-stone streets meet glass-enclosed shopping galleries, where The Freedom Trail landmarks stand beside cutting edge restaurants and renowned performance theatres brush shoulders with trendy nightclubs. Walk the Freedom Trail the first time you visit Boston, and you will quickly get a sense of this coastal city's revolutionary spirit and history. But make sure you also explore some of Boston's fine museums, try the Isabella Stewart Gardner, featuring masterpieces displayed in their collector's mansion, and old neighborhoods like the North End, Boston's Little Italy. You cannot claim to have experienced real Boston culture, though, until you have watched a Red Sox game from the bleachers.

Places to visit when in Boston

a. Freedom Trail

b. Museum of Fine Arts

c. North end

d. Boston public garden

e. Boston Harbor Islands state Park

f. Walk along the city, enjoy it and discover it

San Francisco and Los Angeles

This charming and picturesque West Coast city is the perfect getaway destination for couples, singles, or families. Famous sites, charming streets, beautiful views, and outdoor dining are all part of what makes San Francisco such a great place to visit.

However, I must say San Francisco was not my favorite destination, might have been a rainy weather that did not allow me to enjoy it properly. However, it is a city that worth a short visit if you are around.

Los Angeles is a sprawling Southern California city and the center of the nation’s film and television industry. Near its iconic Hollywood sign, studios such as Paramount Pictures, Universal and Warner Brothers offer behind-the-scenes tours. On Hollywood Boulevard, TCL Chinese Theatre displays celebrities’ hand- and footprints, the Walk of Fame honors thousands of luminaries and vendors sell maps to stars’ homes.

Miami is a city that will capture you with its dazzling glamor and hold you in its spell. The beauty of the city’s nature will also steal your heart from Biscayne Bay and the clear blue South Florida skies.

Washington

The capital city of the United States it is a worth place to the visit if you are staying in NYC, for instance, or even to plan it as another destination in your trip. Easy to get to by train, bus or by car, and of course, by plane. Do not miss its museums and monuments: the famous Smithsonian, all of them! and the National Mall.

Washington is a compact city on the Potomac River, bordering the states of Maryland and Virginia. It is defined by imposing neoclassical monuments and buildings, including the iconic ones that house the federal government’s 3 branches: the Capitol, White House and Supreme Court

With so much to see in this vast country, it can be difficult to know where to start when it comes to planning a trip in the United States. World-class cities, some known for history and others known for fun or glamour, give visitors a broad spectrum of places to choose from. The best places to visit in the United States may simply depend on your personal interests.

Best traditional must-try foods in the USA

Apple pie The simple combination of sugar, buttery pastry and tart sliced apples produces a dessert so extraordinary people have devoted their entire lives to perfecting it.

The hamburger Of course you can have it at home, but only one place is recognised by the Library of Congress as being the birthplace of hamburgers: New Haven, Connecticut.

Clam Chowder It is basically illegal to visit Boston without trying New England clam chowder. The fragrant soup is sold everywhere, and it looks hideous, being white and lumpy.

Bagel and Lox Traditionally consists of an open-faced bagel topped with a generous smear of plain cream cheese, thinly sliced red onions and capers and lox

Deep dish Pizza Pizza in Chicago looks and tastes different

Biscuit A biscuit in America means, essentially, a flaky scone often made with lard and buttermilk

Hominy grits Which is essentially corn milled into a rough powder and then boiled up with butter or bacon grease. It sounds rough but it's actually sublime

Tacos Los Angeles is a city with a taqueria on every street corner, basically. With so many Spanish-speakers it's possible to find anything from greasy nachos on Venice Beach to exquisite Michoacan style goat stews.

Grand Canyon To experience some of the most inspiring views America has to offer, plan a trip to Grand Canyon National Park

Yellowstone America's first national park provides more than 900 miles of hiking trails, as well as plenty of attractions to excite nature lovers, from steaming geysers to bubbling hot springs.

Yosemite A vacation at Yosemite National Park in California is all about reconnecting with nature.

Maui With idyllic beaches and verdant parks, Maui offers ample opportunities to relax

New Orleans Few American cities are home to cultures as unique as this city

Aspen Skiing is the primary activity of choice for most visitors when visiting this destination

Las Vegas Think of his place more as a theme park rather than as a city
