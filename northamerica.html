North America

Let's watch a video to get inspired about what it is, in my personal opinion, the most amazing destination in the US: New York

Well, what could I tell you about the USA that you do not know already? It is an amazing country, with beautiful places, however, I will focus on the cities that, again in my opinion, are worth visiting

New York

Known as the Big Apple, this is a city that will leave you completely marvelous: a cosmopolitan city, crowed everywhere, with so many cool places to visit and enjoy, always growing and evolving, with big iconic landmarks and towering skyscrapers. It is a city where you can experience a vibrant culture all around, with awesome neighborhoods and boroughs to walk and discover.
