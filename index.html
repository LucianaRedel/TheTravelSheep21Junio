The travel sheep

There might be thousand of hundreds of web pages for traveling advice, the intention of this one is not to be the same. The idea is one, though, is to have a collaborative space where you could find some useful information and where the locals could help tourists with their knowledge and experience, and where tourist help other tourists as well. We would like to be the link between locals and visitors too, maybe even touring them around and enjoying some time together, learning from each other, helping us each other, sharing our love and passion for traveling.

I love to travel, and if you feel the same, this webpage will help you and give you the best tips to travel anywhere you want and make your travelling dreams come true.

From my personal experience, the most important thing is to plan as much as you can, traveling is not cheap, and we want to get the best of it for sure!

For some reason or another, most of all my traveling adventures have been on my own. But of course, that has not stopped me from pursuing my dreams of visiting all those places I have seen on tv and internet for years. I must recognize that I am probably weird in that sense, but come on, not doing something you want to do just because it scares you, or because you will do it alone, is not the way to living life for me!

And it has not been easy either, I live in a beautiful country, but where everything is more complicated than it should be....So never forget it, not matter how much time or effort it might takes you, if you can imagine it, if it is what makes you feel alive, if you can dream it, you can do it, just believe in yourself, and do not let anything to stop you!

Travel makes us modest; we see how insignificant tiny we are, expands our mind. I cannot think of anything that excites a greater sense of childlike wonder than to be in a country where we are ignorant of almost everything, and suddenly we are 5 years old again: we cannot read it all, we cannot understand it all, we have a rudimentary sense of how things work there, we are learning everything once over again, and it feels fantastic to discover the world, to look everything with different eyes.


My personal advice, and which for me is the most important of all, take the time to put your camera away and gaze in wonder of the beauty of the place, of the experiences you are having, that is what it will fill your soul, that will make you feel alive, that will move you, and instead of having a pile of photos to show, you will have a moving experience to tell about.


That is the magic of traveling!!
